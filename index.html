GW CS Senior Design 2017

Katherine Walker: Brain Connectivity

I am measuring the connectivity of brains using small-worldness and then correlating connectivity with demographic data, such as stress and memory.

Bio: Katherine Walker is a senior in the Department of Computer Science. She is currently researching digital geometry and is interning with Etsy as a Security Intern. Katherine is the co-chair of Women in Computer Science and is launching a mentorship program for female computer science students at GW. During the summer of 2016, she interned with Etsy in Brooklyn, NY. She interned with Visa in Austin, TX during the summer of 2015. Katherine is from Charlotte, North Carolina.
